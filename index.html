    History of Locus Stationary

    In the early 2000s, when there were no CD/DVD stores even near the Behala Sodepur area, Mrs. Sumi Samaddar decided that it was essential to have a CD/DVD store in the area so as to make the tech available to the people living in the area. So, she founded Locus!

    But as the popularity of CDs & DVDs went down, Locus steadily switched to selling stationary, pens, pencils, gift items & other school and office goodies to its customers.
